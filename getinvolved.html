Becoming an ambassador

Are you a practising artist? Have you ever considered facilitating workshops in schools?

At Art Stories, we are looking for passionate ambassadors to promote the achievements of female artists in schools.

What does an ambassador do?

Our ambassadors are placed in a variety of settings, including schools, SEND settings and community projects to faciliate workshops. The role of an ambassador is to educate groups about the role of women artists and their achievements and contributions to Art History. You will also be there to inspire the creation of artwork centred on a given theme tailored to suit the needs of our clients.

What support do ambassadors receive?

We ensure our ambassadors are fully resourced with everything they need, including artist packs containing information on the focus artists for the workshop. All art materials for the workshops are also provided.

How do I become an ambassador?

Simply head over to our contact page and fill out the form. Someone will be in touch within 2 working days to discuss your interest. A CV and DBS checks will be required.
